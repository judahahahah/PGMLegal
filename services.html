Legal Services Focus

We provide dedicated legal assistance in Northern California for the following matters:

  • Wills and Trusts: Drafting comprehensive documents to manage your estate and avoid the costs of probate.
  • Special Needs Trusts: Protecting the financial future of loved ones with disabilities without compromising their public benefits.
  • Power of Attorney: Designating trusted individuals to handle your financial and legal affairs if you are unable to do so.
  • Advance Health Care Directives: Ensuring your medical wishes are known and respected.
  • Trust Administration: Helping trustees navigate the complex legal duties of managing a trust after a passing.
  • Probate: Representing executors and heirs through the court-supervised distribution of assets.
Note: Our services are limited to the jurisdiction of the State of California.
